How it works?

1

Submit a Report

Fill out a short form with details about your lost item, including where you last had it and your contact information.
2

Verificaton Process

Your report will be reviewed by a moderator before it is published in the Lost Items to feed to ensure accuracy and prevent spam.
3

Connect and Communicate

If someone finds or recognizes your item, they will redirected to your social media account to contact you directly.
